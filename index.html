What is BJJ?

Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu is a ground-based martial art built on technique and leverage. Skill beats strength, making it effective for everyone regardless of size.

Our Club

Rolling Thunder Grappling is the only dedicated BJJ and grappling club in Botswana, welcoming complete beginners through to experienced competitors.

Our Story

Founded in Gaborone, we grew from a small passionate group into a competitive club that travels to South Africa for regional tournaments.

Training

What We Train

Classes run three times a week covering fundamentals, all-levels drilling, and open mat sparring. Whether you are stepping on the mat for the first time or preparing for competition, there is a session for you.

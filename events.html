How It Works

Competing for Rolling Thunder

Not sure if you're ready to compete? Here's what you need to know.

Six Months on the Mat

We recommend at least six months of consistent training before entering your first competition. Fundamentals need to be solid.

Speak to Your Coach

Let Lesedi or Lere know you're interested. They will assess your readiness and help you pick the right division and ruleset.

We Travel Together

For South Africa events, the team travels together. Entry fees for qualifying members may be partially subsidised by the club.

Win or Lose, You Learn

Every competition is a learning experience. We review footage together and use the mat feedback to improve training.

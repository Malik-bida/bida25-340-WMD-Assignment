Weekly Timetable

When We Train

Sessions run three times a week. All classes are held at our Gaborone training facility. Beginners are welcome at every session with no prior experience,aside from saturday's class, as that is open sparring.

Show up five minutes early, introduce yourself to the coach, and we'll take care of the rest.

What to Expect

Class Breakdown

Fundamentals + Technique + Sparring

Tuesdays · 18:00

Designed for complete beginners. We cover core positions, escapes, and submissions at a slow, methodical pace. No experience needed, just show up. Drilling and technique for white belts through purple belts. The class has structured rounds with a clear theme so everyone improves together.

All Levels + Sparring

Fridays · 18:00

Same structured drilling as Tuesday, followed by live rounds. Our most popular session, a great way to test what you've been working on.

Open Mat

Saturdays · 09:00

Unstructured sparring and drilling. Come in, warm up, and roll with whoever is on the mat. Perfect for competition prep and personal development.

First Class

What to Bring

Your first two classes are free. Here's everything you need to know before you show up.

Gi or Rash Guard

A BJJ gi or athletic clothing. We train both Gi and No-Gi.

Mouth Guard

Required for sparring sessions. Available at most sports shops.

Water Bottle

Stay hydrated. BJJ is a full-body workout.

Open Mind

Everyone was a white belt once. Leave the ego at the door.
